What gets checked

One scan, seven weighted signals. Every point in your score traces back to a specific, real finding -- not a black box.

Structured data

Validates your schema markup against schema.org and checks whether it's actually complete enough to earn rich results -- not just present.

AI crawler access

Checks whether GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and others are explicitly allowed in, blocked, or just along for the ride by accident.

Content health

Flags content that hasn't been touched in years, and schema that claims content your page doesn't actually contain.

Indexability

Catches noindex tags and canonical mistakes that quietly keep good pages out of search and AI results entirely.

llms.txt & standards

Grades your llms.txt file against the real convention -- a title and zero links isn't the same as a useful one.
